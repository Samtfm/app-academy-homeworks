Honey Ginger Matzah Bars

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

This matzah treat is kosher for Passover, but also delicous any day of the year.

Wikipedia's take on matzah recipies

Ingredients

  • Matzah (crumbled)
  • 1 cup Honey
  • Almonds (chopped)
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Sesame Seeds

Directions

  1. bring honey to a low boil in pot for about a minute (longer for more of a crunch).
  2. Add a generous amoun of ginger and cinnamon. Mix in matzah and almonds.
  3. Spread about a half inch thick onto a non stick sheet. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  4. Cool in the refrigerator. Cut into bars once cool.

This is an old family recipe.
